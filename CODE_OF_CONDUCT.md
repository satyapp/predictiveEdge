# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the PredictiveEdge community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

* Being respectful and considerate.
* Welcoming new contributors.
* Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
* Focusing on what is best for the community.
* Demonstrating empathy toward other community members.
* Sharing knowledge openly.
* Helping others learn and grow.
* Maintaining professional and respectful communication.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment of any kind.
* Trolling or insulting comments.
* Personal or political attacks.
* Public or private harassment.
* Publishing another person's private information without permission.
* Disruptive or intentionally harmful behavior.
* Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional community.

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, documentation, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including but not limited to:

* GitHub repositories
* Discussions
* Issues
* Pull requests
* Documentation
* Community forums
* Chat platforms
* Conferences or community events representing PredictiveEdge

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to the project maintainers.

Until a dedicated project email is established, open a private GitHub discussion with the maintainers or use the contact method published in the repository.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled respectfully and confidentially where possible.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

* Private warning
* Public warning
* Temporary suspension
* Permanent ban from community spaces

Actions will be based on the severity and frequency of violations.
