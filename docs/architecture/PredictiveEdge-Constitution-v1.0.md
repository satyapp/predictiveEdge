# PredictiveEdge Constitution v1.0

**Project:** PredictiveEdge  
**Tagline:** **Trade Smarter. Stay in Control.**  
**Description:** PredictiveEdge is an open-source AI Trading Intelligence Platform that helps traders make transparent, explainable, and data-driven decisions while remaining broker-independent.

## Mission
To build the world's most trusted open-source AI trading intelligence platform that empowers every trader with transparent, explainable, and broker-independent decision support, while fostering a global community of developers, researchers, and traders.

## Vision
To become the world's leading open-source AI trading intelligence ecosystem, enabling anyone to build, extend, and benefit from institutional-grade trading technology.

## Core Philosophy
> Brokers execute trades. PredictiveEdge provides intelligence.

## Human-in-Control
> AI recommends. The trader decides.
